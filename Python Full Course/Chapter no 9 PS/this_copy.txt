AI (Artificial Intelligence) data science ek interdisciplinary field hai jo machine learning aur statistical techniques ka use karke data se insights nikalta hai.
Yeh field large datasets ko analyze karne, patterns identify karne, aur future predictions banane mein madad karti hai.
Data scientists AI models train karte hain jisse computers human jaise decisions le sakte hain.
AI tools jaise neural networks, decision trees, aur clustering algorithms kaafi popular hote ja rahe hain.
Aaj ke digital yug mein, AI data science har industry mein decision-making ko smarter aur faster bana raha hai.
